How to play?

  1. Select a square to start.
  2. The next moves that you can do are:
  3.   · Horizontaly & Vertical: leaving 2 squares in white.
  4.   · Diagonaly: leaving 1 square in white.
  5. The goal? Try to fill all the squares!
  6. GOOD LUCK!
